[h1]Silkworm Farm[/h1]

Tired of dangerous jungle expeditions just to snag some silk? Ever dreamed of being a silkworm ranch owner? Always wanted adorable wriggly pets that actually pay rent? Then this mod is absolutely for you!

Build your very own Silk Farm where these little silk-spinning superstars munch on mulberry and reward you with precious silk thread! Perfect for mountain folk who enjoy the high life - both altitude-wise AND luxury-wise!

[hr][/hr]

[h2]What You Get[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Silk Farm Building:[/b] Converts mulberry into silk thread and caught silkworms (1 daily silk thread + 0.1 daily caught silkworms per mulberry)
[*] [b]Catch Animal Ability:[/b] Catch wild silkworms in the wilderness
[*] [b]Silkworm Cocoons:[/b] Craft caught silkworms into cocoons (requires Agriculture skill level 16)
[*] [b]Silk Thread Production:[/b] Process cocoons to get 3-6 silk threads each
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]How It Works[/h2]
[list]
[*] Find wild silkworms and catch them using the Catch Animal ability
[*] Build a Silk Farm (requires 4 silkworm cocoons and 2 resting places, minimum 20 area)
[*] Feed mulberry to your farm to produce silk thread and more caught silkworms
[*] Craft caught silkworms into cocoons for even more silk production
[*] Enjoy your spider-free, jungle-free silk empire!
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Important Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*] Requires the [b]core_2[/b] mod to function properly
[*] Works only for people who have mountain-based town with mulberry naturally available as a resource - silkworms love to chew on some mulberry leaves
[/list]
